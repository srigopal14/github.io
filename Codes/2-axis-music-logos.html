2 Axis Music Logos: Creative Typography & Color

Project Overview

I designed a music logo for a brand called "Axis Music". The concept was to integrate music notes into letterforms, creating a playful and unique brand identity that combines musical structure with visual creativity.

Design & Execution

  • Conceptual Design: The logo merges music notes with letters to create a memorable and imaginative brand mark.
  • Typography: One logo uses a clean, professional font, while the "music" element in the creative version features a cursive style for artistic emphasis.
  • Color Palette: Vibrant, playful colors enhance energy and visual impact.
  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator for scalable, high-quality graphics.
